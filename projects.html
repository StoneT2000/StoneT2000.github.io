Polytomizator

This is a website that helps you create poly art very quickly instead of spending countless hours creating triangles on Photoshop/Illustrator. At the moment, it only works on Firefox and Chrome, and it is also highly suggested to try this out on Firefox as it is much faster. More details can be found at its repository.

The image above was created within 10 to 20 seconds, wheras it might take you possibly 5 or more hours to make on photoshop. All work can be downloaded as a high resolution png image or a .svg file.

Simple Screensaver

A minimalist site that serves as a simple color screensaver with color palletes based on the 4 seasons: spring, summer, fall, winter.

It's made to have minimal distractions (the footer is even hidden unless you hover your cursor around the bottom of the page).

Cut Bread!

A site where you can test how good you are at cutting bread evenly.

Comes along with a friendly passive-aggresive chef to cheer you on.

Minions!

A site that has a watchful, attentive, and wary, but cute, Minion (From Despicable Me) that watches your every move.

And if you don't like being watched by Stuart, you can change to Kevin, the minion with 2 eyes, by hovering your mouse to the bottom.

Digital Museum

A website concept design on how digital museums should really work. My idea is that you should explore a museum online like you would in real life.

So this means actaully taking an elevator to different floors, and scrolling left and right to walk across a floor.
